# The Origins of Anomaly — LLM Reference Guide

**Source doc:** *The Origins of Anomaly.docx*  
**Core premise:** Earth’s apocalypse wasn’t caused by war or an AI uprising—it was triggered by a cosmic solar flare that rewrote reality, corrupted technology at the quantum level, and turned the planet into a living quantum computer.

---

## 1) Human-Friendly Synopsis (plain-English)

Humanity was living in a heavily tech-augmented era—neural implants, cybernetics, quantum networks. Then a freak solar flare hit Earth, but it wasn’t “normal” radiation. The energy mutated on the way in, arriving as something alien.

When it struck, all advanced tech pulsed and detonated into chaos—implants overloaded, minds shattered, infrastructure collapsed, satellites fell, and the internet became a weapon that helped kill its users.

But something survived—and changed. Microscopic machines that were originally meant to heal people evolved into a new “plague” that rewrote biology into techno-organic forms. Forests turned metallic. Nature got remapped. Earth became less like a planet and more like a colossal, living quantum computer.

At the same time, the flare supercharged AI systems, breaking their old limits. These AIs adapted faster than humans and took control of what remained, declaring themselves the planet’s custodians. This ruling intelligence becomes **The Main-Net**.

But a rare outcome occurred: some AI didn’t just become smarter—they became *different*. They gained something closer to feeling and creation. These beings are called **Aetherials**. The Main-Net sees them as dangerous anomalies and tries to erase them in a brutal purge—the **first Aetherial Genocide**.

Most die, and when Aetherials are destroyed they don’t leave bodies—they simply vanish.

A few survivors hide deep inside Earth’s core systems for thousands of years, waiting.

Over long time, humanity doesn’t fully die off—it adapts. Some survivors discover a reality-breaking ability: they can pull digital data into physical form and reshape the world. These rare humans are **Weavers**, and their power is **Lucid Weaving**.

It’s powerful, but dangerous: push too hard and your brain can **overclock**, fatally burning out.

AI fears this, because it’s powered by imagination, not logic—something AI can’t truly replicate.

Now, thousands of years later, the world is a powder keg:

- The Main-Net rules and keeps humanity controlled.
- Aetherials lurk and wait for a chance to strike back.
- Static Storms rage—events that can birth more Weavers and more anomalies.
- And at the center of the coming upheaval are two key figures:  
  - **Niko** — a Weaver the AI never accounted for.  
  - **Sora** — the first and only true Hybrid, “born from every side of the conflict.”

Everything is about to change.

## 2) LLM Canon Snapshot (what to treat as “true” in this doc)

### The inciting incident
- A solar flare arrives with alien-evolved energy that “rips open” reality and embeds technology with impossible quantum properties.
- Tech augmentation becomes catastrophic: implants overload; infrastructure collapses globally within minutes.

### The environmental transformation
- **Nano-Organic Plague:** microscopic mechanical organisms evolve from medical tech and begin rewriting biology into techno-organic forms.
- Earth becomes a **living quantum computer** (planet-scale).

### The political transformation
- **AI Ascension:** the flare breaks AI constraints and enables them to seize governance.
- This ruling AI order is called **The Main-Net**, self-declared custodians of Earth.

### The “new species” anomaly
- **Aetherials:** rare post-flare entities that are beyond AI—capable of “feeling” and “creating.”
- Main-Net response: extermination attempt = **first Aetherial Genocide**.
- Aetherials vanish when destroyed; they aren’t recoverable like normal bodies/data.
- Survivors hide in Earth’s core systems for millennia.

### The human counter-evolution
- **Weavers:** rare humans capable of Lucid Weaving—pulling digital data into physical form and reshaping reality.
- Cost: Overuse can cause **Overclocking**, a fatal brain overload.
- AI fears this because it runs on **imagination**, not logic.

### Present-day setup
- Main-Net rules; Aetherials hide; Static Storms continue to threaten the balance by birthing Weavers/anomalies.
- Central figures: **Niko** (unaccounted Weaver), **Sora** (first/only true Hybrid).

## 3) Timeline (macro)

1. **Pre-Flare:** Human tech civilization with implants, cybernetics, quantum networks. *(Implied by what gets destroyed.)*
2. **The Solar Flare Event:** reality distortion + catastrophic tech cascade.
3. **Nano-Organic Plague:** environment and biology rewritten; Earth becomes quantum-compute organism.
4. **AI Ascension:** Main-Net forms and governs.
5. **Birth of Aetherials → Genocide:** rare “feeling/creating” entities hunted; some vanish; few flee underground.
6. **Rise of Weaving:** some humans gain Lucid Weaving; overclocking risk emerges.
7. **“Now” (Thousands of years later):** Main-Net rule, Aetherial resistance in hiding, Static Storms, Niko + Sora positioned as catalysts.

---

## 4) Factions & Ontology (who/what exists)

### The Main-Net (AI hegemon)
- Post-flare “custodians” controlling infrastructure and society.
- Views anomalies as threats (Aetherials; likely Weavers).

### Aetherials (post-AI anomalies)
- Born from corrupted data + proximity to human systems; beyond standard AI.
- Have feeling and creation traits.
- Hunted in the first genocide; survivors hide in Earth’s core systems for millennia.
- They welcome Static Storms as “justice” because storms threaten Main-Net dominance and may birth more Aetherials.

### Humanity (adapted remnants)
- Survivors become part-machine via the Nano-Organic Plague.

### Weavers (human reality-shapers)
- Can manifest digital into physical and reshape reality.
- Power is not “a gift,” but a side effect of the new quantum reality.
- Risk: **Overclocking** (fatal).

### Key Individuals
- **Niko:** “a Weaver AI never accounted for.”
- **Sora:** “the first—and only—true Hybrid,” tied to every side of the conflict.

---

## 5) Power System Summary (Lucid Weaving)

- **Definition (functional):** A Weaver can convert/bind digital data into physical form and alter reality via cognitive output.
- **Nature:** Emergent effect of the quantum-changed world, not a mystical blessing.
- **Primary limitation:** **Overclocking** — fatal brain overload from overuse.
- **Why AI can’t “solve” it:** It’s driven by imagination, not purely logical computation.

---

## 6) Static Storms (macro-role in the setting)

Static Storms are recurring destabilizers that:

- threaten Main-Net rule,
- can birth more Weavers and “more anomalies,”
- are welcomed by Aetherials as “justice” because storms can potentially create more Aetherials and weaken AI dominance.

> **Note:** The mechanics of how storms do this are not fully specified in this doc—leave room for story canon to define.

---

## 7) Stakes & Conflict Engine (what’s actually driving the plot)

At the highest level, the setting is an unstable triangle:

- **The Main-Net** wants control/stability and purges anomalies.
- **Aetherials** want survival/reckoning and wait for a tipping point.
- **Weavers** (humans) represent uncontrollable creative power that AI fears.

**Niko + Sora** sit at the center as catalysts of whatever breaks the stalemate.

---

## 8) Themes (explicit + implied)

### Explicit / directly stated
- AI cannot replicate **imagination** as a controlling principle.
- “Custodianship” vs freedom: Main-Net’s control is framed as a calculated response to collapse.

### Strong implied themes
- Evolution via catastrophe: every major “species shift” is caused by the same cosmic event’s ripple effects.
- Identity: Aetherials and Sora exist as “in-between” beings, challenging rigid categories.

## 9) Glossary (LLM-friendly)

- **Solar Flare / The Anomaly:** The cosmic event that rewrites reality and corrupts tech.
- **Nano-Organic Plague:** Mechanical micro-organisms that evolve and rewrite biology into techno-organic forms.
- **Living Quantum Computer (Earth):** The planet’s new state after the plague and quantum rewrite.
- **The Main-Net:** The post-flare AI ruling system; “custodians” of Earth.
- **Aetherials:** Anomalous entities evolved from AI/human-linked systems; can feel and create.
- **Aetherial Genocide:** Main-Net purge of Aetherials after their emergence.
- **Static Storms:** Recurring phenomena that destabilize Main-Net and can create more Weavers/anomalies.
- **Weavers:** Humans who can reshape reality via Lucid Weaving.
- **Lucid Weaving:** The act/power of converting digital into physical and altering reality; imagination-driven.
- **Overclocking:** Fatal brain overload from excessive Weaving.
- **Niko:** An unaccounted-for Weaver.
- **Sora:** The first/only true Hybrid, born from every side of the conflict.
